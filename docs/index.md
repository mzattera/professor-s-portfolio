<h2 id="about">About Me</h2>

I am a Professor of Computer Science at Stanford University, where I have been a faculty member since 2012. My research focuses on the intersection of artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, and computational linguistics.

Prior to joining Stanford, I completed my Ph.D. at MIT under the supervision of Prof. James Henderson, where I developed novel approaches to natural language understanding. I also spent two years as a postdoctoral researcher at Carnegie Mellon University.

<h2 id="research">Research Interests</h2>

**Research Interests:** Artificial Intelligence, Language Models, Natural Language Processing, Information Extraction, Semantic Web, Knowledge Representation and Reasoning, Machine/Deep Learning, Human-AI Collaboration

**Research Sectors (ERC):** PE6_7 - Artificial intelligence, intelligent systems, natural language processing, PE6_10 - Web and information systems, data management systems, information retrieval and digital libraries
