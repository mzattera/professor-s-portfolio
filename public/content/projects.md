# Research Projects

An overview of my current and past research projects. Interested students are encouraged to reach out if any of these areas align with your interests.

---

## Current Projects

### LinguaFlow: Adaptive Language Models

*2022 – Present*

Developing next-generation language models that adapt to individual users' communication styles while maintaining privacy and reducing computational costs.

**Funding**: NSF Grant IIS-2234567  
**Collaborators**: Google Research, MIT CSAIL  
**Students**: Sarah Chen (PhD), Marcus Lee (PhD), Anna Kowalski (MS)

---

### HumanAI: Collaborative Intelligence Systems

*2021 – Present*

Exploring how AI systems can effectively partner with humans in creative and analytical tasks. This project examines the dynamics of human-AI collaboration and develops guidelines for designing AI assistants that truly augment human capabilities.

**Funding**: DARPA, Stanford HAI  
**Collaborators**: Stanford Psychology, Carnegie Mellon HCI  
**Students**: James Rodriguez (PhD), Emily Wang (PhD)

---

### FairNLP: Equitable Language Technology

*2020 – Present*

Addressing bias and fairness in natural language processing systems. We're developing methods to detect, measure, and mitigate various forms of bias in language models and downstream applications.

**Funding**: NSF Grant IIS-1987654  
**Publications**: 12 papers, including 2 best paper awards  
**Open Source**: [github.com/fairnlp](https://github.com)

---

## Completed Projects

### DialogueMaster: Multi-turn Conversation Systems

*2018 – 2022*

Developed advanced dialogue systems capable of maintaining coherent, contextually appropriate conversations across multiple turns. This research contributed to significant improvements in commercial virtual assistants.

**Outcomes**: 8 publications, 3 patents, technology licensed to major tech companies

---

### SemanticWeb: Knowledge Graph Construction

*2015 – 2019*

Automated methods for constructing and maintaining large-scale knowledge graphs from unstructured text. The techniques developed continue to be used in industry applications.

**Outcomes**: 6 publications, open-source toolkit with 2000+ GitHub stars
